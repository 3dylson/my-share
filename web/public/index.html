My Share – Privacy Policy

Effective: 2 May 2025  ·  Last updated: 2 May 2025

⚠️ Not financial advice. My Share is a personal budgeting and planning tool. Nothing in this app constitutes financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Always consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.

1. Who We Are

My Share ("we", "us", "our") is a personal finance planning app for Android. Our privacy contact email is edylson_frederico@outlook.com.

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and your rights over it.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Authentication

  • When you sign in with Google (via Firebase Authentication), we receive your name, email address, and Google profile photo URL. Anonymous sign-in creates a temporary account with no personal identifiers.
  • If you use a local-only account, no authentication data is collected.

Financial Planning Data

  • Your income, fixed costs, savings goals, allocation percentages, and check-in logs that you voluntarily enter into the app.
  • This data is stored in Cloud Firestore (Google Firebase) and associated with your user account. It is never sold or used for advertising targeting.

Usage & Analytics

  • We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised event data (e.g., onboarding steps completed, features used, session duration). This data does not include your financial figures.
  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports and stack traces to improve stability. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of the crash.
  • We use Firebase Performance Monitoring to measure app responsiveness.

Advertising

  • For free users, we display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob uses device identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve contextual or personalised ads depending on your consent choice, which is collected via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP / Consent SDK).
  • Ads are never shown on screens where you enter sensitive financial data.
  • Subscribers and trial users see no ads.

Subscriptions & Purchases

  • Subscription transactions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment card details. We receive a purchase token and subscription status from Google Play, which is verified server-side via a Firebase Cloud Function.

Push Notifications

  • If you opt in to reminders, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send notification tokens to our backend. No financial data is included in push notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your budget plan across sessions.
  • To verify subscription entitlements and unlock premium features.
  • To send check-in reminders you have configured.
  • To improve app stability and performance using aggregated analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, use your financial figures for advertising, or share your data with third parties except as described in Section 4.

4. Third-Party Services

My Share relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics, FCM, Performance, Cloud Functions
  • Google AdMob & UMP — Advertising (free tier only)
  • Google Play Billing — Subscription processing

We do not integrate with any other analytics, marketing, or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

  • Account & Firestore data: Retained while your account exists and for 30 days after deletion to allow recovery.
  • Analytics & Crashlytics: Aggregated event data is retained for up to 14 months per Google's standard retention.
  • Local-only data: Stored in your device's shared preferences and removed when you uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the EU/EEA under GDPR and California under CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent for personalised advertising at any time via app settings.

To delete your account and all associated data, go to More → Account → Delete Account in the app, or email edylson_frederico@outlook.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

My Share is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures including Firebase Security Rules, App Check, and server-side subscription verification to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. Significant changes will also be communicated via an in-app notice.
